# Day 3 - Netflix Movies & TV Shows Analysis

## Objective
Analyze Netflix's catalog to identify trends in content type, countries, genres, and ratings.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

## Analysis Performed
- Explored the dataset
- Checked missing values
- Compared Movies and TV Shows
- Identified the top countries producing Netflix content
- Found the most common genres
- Analyzed content ratings
- Examined the trend of content added over time

## Key Insights
- Movies make up a larger share of Netflix's catalog than TV Shows.
- The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
- Netflix expanded its catalog significantly in recent years.
- Drama and International Movies are among the most common genres.
- TV-MA is one of the most frequent content ratings.
